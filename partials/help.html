Ballot Help
Polls

Polls have a Ballot and a target Voter Roll. They can be started, deleted and even copied as a template.

Ballot

A Ballot is sent to each voter on a Polls Voter Roll. this ballot is then filled out and returned to the Poll.

Voter Roll

Peer Lists

A Peer List is a Contact List that is a one way "mailing list". This is not a group chat.

Group Chat

A Group Chat Voter Roll is made up of all members of the specified Group Chat.

Templates

Poll Templates are pre-defined Polls used for kickstarting a new Poll fast. You can create your own Templates and even Share with Peers.
